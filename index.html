28. —ä

The Hard Sign (—Ç–≤—ë—Ä–¥—ã–π –∑–Ω–∞–∫)

'—ä' is the rarest letter. It has no sound but acts as a separator, making the preceding consonant sound "hard" and distinct from the following vowel. I will mark the separation with an uppercase consonant.

29. —ã

The 'Yery' Sound

'—ã' has a unique sound that doesn't exist in English, similar to a deep, throaty 'i'. I will mark it as 'y'. It never starts a word.

30. —å
